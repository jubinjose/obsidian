
## 🧠 Best “Founder Brain Dump → PRD” Pipeline (My Strong Recommendation)

### 🥇 **Everyday SaaS Workflow (fastest)**

1. Record with Loom
    
2. Copy transcript
    
3. Paste into ChatGPT
    
4. Generate PRD / ADR
    
5. Paste into Confluence / Notion
