

### - **IAM (Identity & Access Management)** $\rightarrow$ _Focus: People & Permissions_
    
### - **Infrastructure Security** $\rightarrow$ _Focus: Things & Systems_
    


### Comparison of the Two Major Groups

| **Feature**          | **IAM (The "Who")**             | **Infrastructure Security (The "What")** |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **What it protects** | Users, Credentials, Roles       | Laptops, Networks, Servers, Data         |
| **Key Question**     | _"Are you allowed to be here?"_ | _"Is the environment safe?"_             |
| **Typical Tools**    | Okta, SailPoint, CyberArk       | CrowdStrike, Netskope, Fortinet, Rubrik  |
| **Analogy**          | **The Key to the House**        | **The Alarm System & Motion Detectors**  |
